What is Concentration?


Concentration is a matching game that will test your memory to its limits. For more information please visit Wikipedia Concentration .

How do I play?

Concentration is normaly played with a deck of cards. Each deck contains 2 cards that share the same symbol. The deck is shuffled and then each card is placed facedown in a grid, one card per grid square. The game rules are as follows:
  1. Player picks two cards.
  2. The two cards are revealed.
  3. If the two cards match symbols, they are left faceup and the player gains a point.
  4. If the two cards are not matching symbols then they are returned facedown.
  5. Game repeats until all matches are found or time runs out.
