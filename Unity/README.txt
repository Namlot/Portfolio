Controls:
WSAD to Move
Left Click to Fire (can be held down)
Right Click to use magic abilities (fire circle or shield)
E to purchase upgrades

Enemies:
Will hurt you by touching them or their bullets
Shoot to destroy

Goal:
Defeat Boss at the end

Cheats:
Press "j" at any time for invincible mode (has a toggle)
Press "r" to restart the level at any time

Notes: Each level will have one curse item and one rapidfire item hidden somewhere

The build provided is a 10 room build that will be random each time with a random boss
